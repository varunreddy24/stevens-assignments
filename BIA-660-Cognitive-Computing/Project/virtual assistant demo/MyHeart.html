My heart

Welcome

We as a developer team, specifically target the healthcare domain and design a cognitive agent that can help each individual assess her/his risk of heart disease. We showed that even a low-cost, simplistic design can pinpoint a person’s risk of heart disease. The system we prototyped here can be extended for more tailored use cases. It can not only be used by the medical practitioners to support their decision-making process but also an individual can leverage the power of the cognitive agent. In the future, this system can be deployed over an application that will provide a better user interface and deeper insights into the problems which may lead to heart disease and more personalized solutions to avoid heart diseases.
